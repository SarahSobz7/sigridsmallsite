Sigrid is a Norwegian singer and songwriter, born Sigrid Solbakk Raabe on September 5, 1996. Her parents are Hakon Raabe and Anette Solberg Solbakk. She has two siblings, and older brother and and older sister. Sigrid rose to stardom in 2013 after she released her first single entitled, Sun. She signed with Island and debuted her first single for the label caleld "Don't Kill My Vibe" in 2017. Sigrid's style can be described as Indie and vibrant. You can find her dancing like nobody's watching in her fun music videos. So far in her career she has releaed 2 successful EP's and followed up with her debut album titled, "Sucker Punch" in 2019.
